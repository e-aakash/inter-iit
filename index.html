  • 7th Inter-IIT Tech Meet 2018.

    IIT BOMBAY

    Inter IIT Tech Meets are envisioned in challenging participants to come up with technology solutions for the problems, compete with teams in a healthy environment, interact with like-minded people across the country and promote transfer of this technology to ventures which will address problems at grass-root levels.

  • Our Vision.

    7th Inter IIT Tech meet caries this vision have collaborative and competitive events promoting these ideas. Competitive events are centred around themes like sustainability, healthcare, agriculture, technology for soldier support, communication technologies for masses, efficient transportation, promotion of indigenous manufacturing etc. In essence all themes try to solutions with social/economic impact and development of local communitie. Apart from the fact that there are 23 IITs, these are in essence institutes with immense research infrastructure located in distinctive geographical locations having problems very specific to region, so technology infusion and enabling students to understand and learn from examples of solutions applicable to different areas is highly accelerated through Inter IIT.
