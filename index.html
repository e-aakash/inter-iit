7th Inter-IIT Tech Meet 2018.

IIT BOMBAY

7th edition of Inter IIT will be happening over 3 days from 18th-20th December 2018 at IIT Bombay. There will be various events planned over the 3 days which can be broadly classified over two categories - collaboration events and competitive events.

Collaboration events include events which foster interaction between participants. The Inter IIT Tech Meet shall serve as a platform for sharing of ideas. Showcase of projects in varied domains being pursued by students across various IITs is one such event.

Teams will be formed by participating IITs which will develop a solution for the problem statement for competitive events and present, demonstrate or display the concept, solution or prototype. They will be evaluated for performance on criteria mentioned along with the problem statement.
